Help Centre

Please see our frequently asked questions below



Q: What are your opening times?

A: 9AM - 6PM Monday to Saturday.

Q: When will my parcel arrive?

A: If you have your TDC Tracking ID/Number, click the Track & Trace button to the right. You'll find out whether your parcel is due for delivery today. We normally deliver between 8a.m. - 6p.m.

Q: I'd like to change my delivery schedule

A: If you’ve received a text or email from us, just click on the Manage Delivery link included in your text or email and let us know through our help form. You can change your delivery date, opt to collect at your local TDC Depot and if the shipper has authorised, your parcel can be delivered to a neighbour.

Q: Do you deliver on Saturdays?

A: We deliver parcels on Saturdays. It is key that you specify that these are to be delivered on Saturday when you request your collection, and use Saturday delivery labels also.

Q: Are my parcels insured?

A: TDC provides standard liability insurance for all parcels sent. Please contact us for more information.

Q: Are there any goods I cannot send?

A: We will always do our best to accommodate your needs. However, for Health & Safety reasons there are some products we cannot carry. They include: Dangerous Goods, Hazardous Goods, Flammable Products, Animals, Perishable Food, Cash, jewellery, precious metals or antiques, liquids.
